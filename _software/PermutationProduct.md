I used this python script to compute product of permutations when I was learning abstract algebra / 过去我学习抽象代数时使用这段python代码计算置换乘积

Example:
```
Under group S_?:6
(1 2 3 4)(2 3 4 5)
(1 2 4 5 3)(6)
(2 3 4 5)(1 2 3 4)
(1 3 5 2 4)(6)
(1 2 3)(2 4)(1 3 2)
(1)(2)(3 4)(5)(6)
(1 2 3 4 5 6)(1 3 5 2 4)(6 5 4 3 2 1)
(1)(2 4 6 3 5)
```
